Campaign Planning

Plan your D&D campaign with these resources and templates.

Worldbuilding Checklist

  • Setting: Define the world (e.g., high fantasy, grimdark).
  • Factions: List major groups (e.g., guilds, kingdoms).
  • History: Create a timeline of key events.
  • Locations: Describe major cities, dungeons, and landmarks.

Sample Campaign Hook

The Crimson Veil: A mysterious cult is spreading a magical plague. The party must uncover its source before the kingdom falls.
